Community leaderboard

Community-submitted runs on the 46-task Long-Horizon Terminal-Bench suite, ranked by mean reward. The seed entries are our Terminus-2 baselines — the same runs analyzed in the report. LHTB is open and follows the same submission flow as Terminal-Bench 2: run the 46 tasks with five trials each under the same 90-minute budget, then open a pull request with your job directories. A bot validates the submission and a maintainer reviews the trajectories against the hidden verifiers before it lands on the board. A verified badge means we reproduced it.
