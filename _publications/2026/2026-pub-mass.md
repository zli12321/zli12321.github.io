abstract: >-
  We present MASS, a motion-aware spatial-temporal grounding framework that enhances vision-language models' ability to reason about physical phenomena. By explicitly modeling motion dynamics and spatial-temporal relationships, MASS improves VLM performance on physics-based reasoning and comprehension tasks that require understanding of object motion, forces, and physical interactions.
