abstract: >-
  We propose Graph of Skills (GoS), an inference-time structural retrieval layer for large skill libraries. GoS constructs an executable skill graph offline from skill packages, then at inference time retrieves a bounded, dependency-aware skill bundle through hybrid semantic-lexical seeding, reverse-aware Personalized PageRank, and context-budgeted hydration, solving the prerequisite gap that leaves semantically retrieved skills execution-incomplete.
