abstract: >-
  We introduce COS-PLAY, a co-evolution framework where LLM-based decision agents and skill bank agents mutually improve each other for long-horizon task completion. The decision agent learns to leverage an evolving skill bank, while the skill bank adapts based on the decision agent's performance, enabling continual improvement on complex, multi-step tasks requiring multi-step reasoning, skill chaining, and robust decision-making under delayed rewards.
